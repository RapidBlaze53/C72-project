School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
We Won first prize in the Inter School Debate on Environment Pollution

This was an award for our debate about pollution in the environment, with some people thinking that it would help the world progress, cleaning it later on, while others thought that it would utterly destroy the world if not taken care of right now. It got so heated, to the point where we got first prize in the Inter School Debate on Environment Pollution

Inter School Football Competition
We Came Second in the inter school football Competition

We simply played football a lot, practiced a lot, and reached the finals. The opposing team made a lot of fouls, and it went unnoticed, and due to the unfairness of the game, they won and we got second.

Inter School Science Project Competition
We Won Second Prize in the Inter School Science Project Competition

Everyone in the school made a science project with their class, and the best one got picked to be judged against other schools science projects. We got second, as we lost to a representation of an atom, as we did animal biology.
